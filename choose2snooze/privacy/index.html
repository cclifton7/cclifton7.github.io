Choose2Snooze — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Choose2Snooze ("the app") is a flexible alarm app for iPhone and Apple Watch. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect. In plain terms: Choose2Snooze does not have its own servers, does not run third-party analytics or advertising, and does not sell or share your data.

Alarms and settings you create

The alarms, schedules, sounds, and preferences you set are stored on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Music and your media library

With your permission, Choose2Snooze can use a song from the music already on your device as an alarm sound. The app reads your local media library only to let you choose a song, and audio is played locally on your device. The app does not upload, copy, or transmit your music.

Audio you record

Choose2Snooze lets you record your own sound to use as an alarm. Recordings are saved on your device and are never uploaded or sent to the developer.

HomeKit

Optionally, an alarm can trigger a HomeKit scene (for example, turning on your lights) when it fires. If you use this feature, the app requests Home access to run the scene you choose. This controls your own home and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer of Choose2Snooze cannot see your home data.

Alarm alerts and notifications

Choose2Snooze uses Apple's AlarmKit to deliver prominent alarm alerts. These alerts are scheduled and generated on your device and are not sent through any server.

Update checks

To let you know when a new version is available, the app may fetch a small public file listing the latest version number. This request contains no personal information and is not used to identify you.

Data the developer collects

None. Choose2Snooze has no backend server and the developer receives no personal data from your use of the app, except information you choose to send directly (for example, if you email feedback or a bug report).

Children's privacy

Choose2Snooze is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
